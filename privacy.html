Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Acmerea POD Canvas Radar (“the App”) handles information in connection with its internal research and reporting use.

1. Who we are

Acmerea POD Canvas Radar is an internal business tool operated by Acmerea Capital for print-on-demand theme monitoring, reporting, and decision support.

2. Information we may process

The App may process limited information such as:

  • Account-related authorization data required to access approved platform APIs
  • Selected content or trend signals retrieved through authorized integrations
  • Internal report data, theme labels, and research outputs
  • Basic technical logs needed for reliability, troubleshooting, and workflow monitoring

3. How we use information

We use information only for legitimate internal business purposes, including:

  • Monitoring trends relevant to POD research
  • Organizing and analyzing product-theme signals
  • Generating internal summaries and reports
  • Improving internal workflows and decision support

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is not shared with third parties except as necessary to operate approved integrations, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and security, or support internal technical operations.

5. Data storage and retention

Data may be stored in internal systems or approved service providers only for as long as reasonably necessary to support research, reporting, security, and operational needs.

6. Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Third-party services

The App may rely on authorized third-party platforms, APIs, and infrastructure providers. Their separate terms and privacy policies may also apply where relevant.

8. Your choices

Where applicable, you may contact us regarding questions about data handling, correction requests, or deletion-related inquiries, subject to legal and operational limitations.

9. Contact
